Who We Are

The Philly Golf Pops is a spirited group of senior golfers at the Philadelphia Country Club who believe age is just a number — and a great excuse for a mulligan.

We play regularly, enjoy friendly competition, share plenty of laughs, and occasionally remind the younger members that experience still beats youth on most days.

Whether you're looking for a regular foursome, a little side-action betting, or just good company after the round, you've found your people.
